Useless Bot

Useless Bot is a discord bot that can help you in some sort of way!
Useless Bot can tell you the current weather and forecast of a area!
Useless Bot can search and play music!
Useless Bot can tell you jokes!
Useless Bot has memes!
Useless Bot can get images from reddit
Useless Bot can give you minecraft player or server information
Useless Bot has games like Minesweeper, slots, rock paper scissors and more!
Useless Bot also has a economy system where everytime you chat you earn money!

Useless Bot started as a project during the pandemic.
I was bored and I wanted to learn how to program.
Working on this for over 7 months I learned a lot
But i did not expect Useless Bot to be in 1,000 Server!
Now Useless Bot is a fun hobby of mine!
